Our Objectives

1. To analyze the diverse aspects related to roti canai, including its variations, preparation methods, economic factors, and health implications.

2. To archive the cultural significance and popularity of roti canai in Malaysian society.

This project aims to explore and archive various aspects of roti canai, including its types, preparation methods, economic value, health considerations, and its cultural importance within Malaysian society.
